About Me

Hello! I'm Raftar, a self-made Free Fire player with over 5 years of experience. My journey began during the COVID-19 lockdown, and what started as a casual interest quickly turned into a passion.

I initially played on mobile, spending a long time mastering the basics. Over time, I transitioned to PC gaming, where I explored and optimized various settings to enhance performance. I now play on a mid-range PC and have spent countless hours fine-tuning everything — from Windows optimizations to emulator configurations. I've worked through lag, delay, aim shake, joystick glitches, and many bugs, gradually building a smooth and responsive gameplay experience.

Throughout the years, I've made great friends in the game and enjoyed pushing ranks as a team. One thing I’ve always wished for is a true squad — a group that understands each other, plays with synergy, and grows together. While I’ve played with many skilled players, I'm still on the lookout for that perfect team dynamic.

This is just a glimpse into my journey with Free Fire — a game that taught me patience, strategy, and the joy of continuous improvement.
